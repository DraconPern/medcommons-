  Pricing  
  Payment is not required if you have a valid Promo Code. During the testing period the Promo Code is WELCOME.


Upload DICOM CD and share with a Voucher
Anyone is free to create a HealthURL and preview the contents on the same computer. The HealthURL will be automatically deleted after 30 days. Sharing the HealthURL using the Voucher requires a one-time payment of $10. Payment can be made by the sender before communicating the Voucher to the recipient. Otherwise, the recipient will be required to make the payment prior to setting their password to access the HealthURL.

Payment starts a 30-day timer after which the HealthURL will be deleted. Payment allows authorized users to share, add documents and DICOM and otherwise collaborate via the HealthURL until it expires.

MedCommons accepts only Amazon Payments at this time.


Practice Group
Practice Group registration is free but using the Group costs $10 per 30-day HealthURL. Any Group member can add HealthURL credits to the Group by signing in and using their Settings tab. The group's account will be debited the first time each HealthURL is accessed from the group's patient list. HealthURLs will be automatically deleted after 30 days.

Users of the group's Private Upload Form will not be challenged for payment and can check their HealthURL on the same computer they use to upload.

Practice Group access is free during this initial testing period.
