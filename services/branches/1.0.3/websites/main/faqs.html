  MedCommons Platform FAQs:

How large and small a system can I gemerate?
In a small implementation MedCommons will appeal to specialist groups that are installing sophisticated imaging equipment but lack the IT expertise for digital image management. In a large implementation MedCommons can connect many thousands of facilities together into a large network.

What level of effort is required to utilize the Platform?.
To make it a snap to embed MedCommons into your own healthcare solution, we supply out of the box features for instantly integrating radiology systems and specialty workstations. You are able to connect your remote facilities from your central location on demand and incrementally. Your normal operations staff can monitor our standards based server infrasturcture.

Who are the customers for the medCommons Platform?
We expect interest in the platform from integrators with vastly different goals and capabilities:

  • innovative medical practices that write their own database-driven web application and use us to transport, display and store standard medical documents including DICOM,
  • consumer and physician-facing web services that use health records as input to a care management or decision support application to facilitate handling and display of standard medical records,
  • systems integrators that want to store medical documents in an enterprise archive independent of any particular application vendor to save money on storage and avoid vendor lock-in
