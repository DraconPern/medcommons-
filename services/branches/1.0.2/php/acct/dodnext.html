How to Use Your HealthURL

Use your uploaded data in one of the following ways:

  • Phone, email, text or fax the Voucher ID and PIN to the recipient
  • Register a practice group to avoid the need for emails
  • Fax in documents behind a bar-coded cover sheet
  • Add scanned documents in PDF format
  • Pre-pay so your recipient won't need to pay (free with Promo Code during beta)
