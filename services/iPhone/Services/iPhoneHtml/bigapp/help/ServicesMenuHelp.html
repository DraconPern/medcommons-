Services Menu Help

You will see several offers for services based around submitting or sharing your PhotoSets with other people and services.

These services will change from time to time, even without an upgrade to your iPhone application.

Services for fee are charged thru the iTunes App Store. All Terms of Service of the iTunes App Store will apply

Removing Services From the Menu

You can remove entire clusters of services on your Main Settings Page

You can re-enable services at any time

Requesting Service Execution

Select a PhotoSet from your Vault, Hit the Services Button and Choose the Service from the List

You may be prompted to supply more information as needed by the particular service

Receiving Notifications

Depending on the Service you have selected, you may receive a Notifcation in the Incoming Notifications tab

Payment is thru the iTunes App Store. You can download your entire Foto Vault and leave the service at any time.
