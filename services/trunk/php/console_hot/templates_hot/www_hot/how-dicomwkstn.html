How To: Connect a DICOM Workstation

last updated on Sep 9,2010

         
    Click Configure DDL
You can start DDL by clicking the Upload DICOM CD button 		 
         
    Enter the DICOM parameters for your workstation
These are usually found in a preferences menu. 		 
         
    Choose CSTORE export  
         
    Click Update Configuration
 
         
    Check for a DICOM Echo  
         
    Test your connection
DICOM files are large and could take minutes to transfer. Mouse over the DDL icon in your system tray to monitor progress.
