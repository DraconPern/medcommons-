  Doctors and patients can upload and share MRI's, CT's in just a few minutes - eliminate the need for physical transportation of radiology CDs! The CD information is transfered into a secure online MedCommons HealthURL, a standard health record with the full quality DICOM images attached. Absolutely no typing is required, the patient name and other information are read directly from the CD. See a sample HealthURL here.  
   
 

How It Works:

We install a DICOM CD reader on your Windows, Mac or Linux PC.

You tell the reader the location of your CD.
The reader uploads the CD; full quality radiology CDs can be large, you may preview the HealthURL and images even as the upload proceeds.
You will receive a VoucherID code and PIN that you can share with anyone to grant access to the HealthURL.
Save the VoucherID and PIN, share as you wish; after payment the access is good for 30 days.

  • Manage health records for an entire family
  • iPhone and smartphone support for key viewing and sharing features
