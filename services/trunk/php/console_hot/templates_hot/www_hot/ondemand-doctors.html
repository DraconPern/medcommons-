  Doctors and patients can upload and share MRI's, CT's in just a few minutes - eliminate the need for physical transportation of radiology CDs! The CD information is transfered into a secure online MedCommons HealthURL, a standard health record with with the full quality DICOM images attached. Absolutely no typing is required, the patient name and other information are read directly from the CD. See a sample HealthURL here.  
   
 

How It Works:

We install a DICOM CD reader on your Windows, Mac or Linux PC.

You tell the reader the location of your CD.
The reader uploads the CD; full quality radiology CDs can be large, you may preview the HeathURL and images even as the upload proceeds.
You will receive a VoucherID code and PIN that you can share with anyone to grant access to the HealthURL.
Save the VoucherID and PIN, share as you wish; after payment the access is good for 30 days.
Authorized users can view the HealthURL, including images in a Web browser. In addition, radiologists and surgeons can connect their DICOM workstation to the HealthURL. Electronic health records that support Web links can also display the HealthURL.

BENEFITS FOR DOCTORS

  • Display a simple unified view of a patients medical record with linked images and documents
  • A collaborative workspace for each patient's care team including other practices and family members
  • Easily assemble information from diverse internal and external practices
  • Expand the referral base without any up-front connection cost
  • Radiologists and surgeons can complement a favorite radiology workstation. Free open source workstations are available for both Mac and Windows
  • iPhone and smartphone access to images, reports, documents anywhere
