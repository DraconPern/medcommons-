APP DESCRIPTION

MedCommons Prototyper is a free utility to assist designers and developers building SplitView layouts on the iPad.

Prepare customized, networked iPad presentations using MedCommons Prototyper to test your HTML5 and Ajax designs under the iPad UISplitViewController.

Test re-sizing and rotation issues for different document types displayed as WebViews

Presentations are closed and limited to just the Web content you choose to expose.

Sample Plist templates can be copied and modified and shared for group collaboration.

MedCommons Prototyper is used by MedCommons and its customer to produce customized ipad solutions for Healthcare.


Graphics by Martoons

 
Features:

  • A simple network-based Plist configures MedCommons Prototyper Utitlity (specifications)

  • As per the Apple HIG, there is one outer SplitView Controller created.

  • Rotational and popover behavior is as presented by IB generated code.

  • The Prototyper Plist drives the detailed layout of the button configurations for the Master and Detail Controllers.

  • Multiple scene support in the Plist allows navigation between different prototype layout

  • Sample templates ready for your modification.
