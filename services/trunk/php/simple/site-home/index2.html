Coming Soon: NHIN

The National Health Information Network is a set of standards, services and policies that enable secure health information exchange over the Internet and support an easy "on-ramp" for a wide set of providers and organizations looking to adopt. At the conclusion of this project, there will be one nationwide exchange, consisting of the organizations that have come together in a common policy framework to implement the standards and services. This project is open government, and as such, contains avenues for a broad range of public participation.
