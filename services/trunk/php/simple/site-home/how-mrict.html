How To: Connect an MRI/CT Scanner

last updated on August 9,2010

         
    Click Configure DDL
You can start DDL by clicking the Upload DICOM CD button 		 
         
    Note DDL DICOM Parameters
You will need to enter these into the CT, MR or workstation that will send images to your HealthURLs. 		 
         
    Add DDL to your DICOM device
Some vendors lock these settings. This may require a call to your administrator or service technician. 		 
         
    Select the images and choose Export
Every DICOM device does this a bit differently. You will be asked to select DDL or the AETitle you entered for DDL. 		 
         
    Check your Patient List
A HealthURL with the images will be at the top of the list.
