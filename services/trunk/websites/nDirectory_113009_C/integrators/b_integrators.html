MedCommons is for systems integrator managed health records storage.

MedCommons enables systems integrators to combine cloud and advanced storage architectures to deliver a standards-based, vendor-neutral health records archive for institutions with multiple sites and diverse vendors. The MedCommons platform adds patient access, ARRA accounting for disclosures along with diagnostic imaging and document capability for multi-site enterprise and National Health Information Network (NHIN) integrations.

Picture Archiving and Communications Systems (PACS) are the essential, high-speed network that connects images and reports among scanners, workstations, storage, workflow and electronic health records (EHR) systems. Advances in cloud computing speed and reliability increasingly make it possible to move PACS and other storage intensive systems to secure and efficient commodity infrastructure.
