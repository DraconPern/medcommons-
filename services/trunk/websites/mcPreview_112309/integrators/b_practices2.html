The National Health Information Network (NHIN) is the federally mandated interchange for hospitals and other institutions that hold health records on behalf of patients. Although most electronic health records (EHR) vendors are expected to provide NHIN functionality, the management of diagnostic imaging and scanned documents by doctors, direct access to health records by patients and patient-controlled Web services are most efficiently managed by

MedCommons uses the mandated standard interfaces to one or many EHRs in conjunction with standard single signon and security protocols to provide flexible and robust communications for better patient care.

MedCommons is designed for practices of all sizes that want to access health records from participating hospitals and labs.
