  The MedCommons Platform provides a standards-based, scalable document management architecture for secure communications and storage of health records and imaging over the Internet and within the enterprise, with policy based controls for Patients and Provider. The Platform can be customized by end user and /or systems integrator for workflow and decision support. Built-in connections to Google Health, MS HealthVault and common Portals add value to each Health Record Account by integrating many diverse sources and document types all under your control.

MedCommons Accounts support standard health records and other documents, including DICOM imaging, XML, and custom document types. Every Access is audited and controlled by Consents and Policies managed by either Providers, Patients, or the Service Operator. Modern web protocols for document and service access, integrated Web2.0 notifications and gadget support for Portals, and strong Ajax and iPhone interface support provides a foundation for advanced Healthcare Applications.

Integration of MedCommons into your application ecosystem is a matter of choosing storage and other data center related options and performance features. Wiring your network of of hospitals and imaging centers to participate in the MedCommons network is as easy as a quick download and communications test to verify connectivity. Integration with your Patient Portal or with iGoogle is a snap with our ready to go gadgets.

The MedCommons Platform includes a scalable message switch with interfaces to important HealthCare Services and Social Networks and the ubiquitous Fax network. The MedCommons DDL component integrates hospital and imaging center facilities into the platform, which also services as the collaborative interface into vendor neutral archival storage. The platform scales as needed with the number of users by adding additional Gateways, and increasing the size of the MySQL database. Systems Integrators can extend the platform with custom workflow and decision support integration.

MedCommons Platform provides extreme configuration flexibility for the smallest and largest networks. As a packaged VmWare or Amazon AMI Appliance, the platform slips into your existing enterprise or cloud infrastructure. As your service grows, additional Appliance instances can be added as needed. For deeper integration, some MySQL tables and documents can be directly accessed by your application. MedCommons supports Systems Integrators with consultants for custom software interfaces.

MedCommons monitors all instances hosted by us at Amazon, and can monitor your service too. Our 24 hour help desk is available for problem resolution on your network. We can monitor and adjust the capacity of your network as needed based upon measured demand. All of the MedCommons Products, and any applications developed by Systems Integrators, take advantage of these services to provide a quick head start to your team.

Systems Integrators can utilize our custom iPhone and other web apps as building blocks for their comprehensive healthcare solutions.
