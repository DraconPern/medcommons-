APP DESCRIPTION
BigApple Shooter is a free personal photography application and web service that connects your iPhone to several NYC and national services that improve the quality of life for your family and your community.

Your photos and videos are hermetically sealed in the cloud for one year and are immediately and exclusively available to you and for sharing with NYC Agencies, Child Safety organizations, Homeland Security, your healthcare providers, and friends and family.

View Lists and Maps of Places to Avoid - mined from the NYC.gov database, and use BigApple Shooter to add more places to avoid!

BigApple Shooter is the MedCommons contest entry for the 2009 NYC BigApps Competition
Graphics by Martoons

     
Features:
  • Keep your family safe by collecting photos, videos and documents on your iPhone for emergency purposes and for presenting as evidence of problems requiring attention

  • File Reports with NYC311 Service for reporting on Building Grafﬁti, Damaged Trees, Dirty Vacant Lights, Street Lights, Signs, Conditions, and Lost Property in Taxis

  • View Restaurants and Other Facilities to Avoid Based on NYC Data

  • Report on Any Other Problems in the City by collecting forensic quality photos and videos with GPS and sharing them with other 3rd parties

  • Shoot photo or video on your iPhone 3G

  • Capture photos from carmera roll or photo albums

  • Shoot ofﬂine, automatically synchronizes when back in range

  • Share with simple ID and PIN scheme

  • Connect anonymously or with full identity

  • New services can be added to the iPhone under the control of the New York City
