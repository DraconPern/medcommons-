How It Works:

Register your practice with MedCommons, fill in a simple form to provide your contact name and email, and the email addresses for anyone else in your practice you'd like to have access. For HIPAA compliance, each invited individual will be sent a separate registration to provide their own password.

A private Inbox is created for your practice, and we provide a custom Web Link that can be shared with anyone for sending in DICOM CDs - just email the link, post it on your website, whatever you wish.
When uploaded, the CD information is transferred into a secure online MedCommons HealthURL, a standard health record with the full quality DICOM images attached.
The practice members that you invite can view the HealthURLs in your private inbox using their individual passwords, and outside CD submitters can access only the HealthURLs that they submitted.
Authorized users can view the HealthURL, including images in a Web browser. In addition, radiologists and surgeons can connect their DICOM workstation to the HealthURL. Electronic health records that support Web links can also display the HealthURL.  

BENEFITS FOR PRACTICES

  • Easy integration into practice websites and custom databases
  • Effective collaboration platform for clinicians and outsourced services
  • Preload diagnostic images directly from a patients radiology CD
  • Deliver radiology images to a surgeons desktop, workstation, operating room or iPhone
  • Save time for both patients and staff by posting a health record upload form pre-addressed to your practice
