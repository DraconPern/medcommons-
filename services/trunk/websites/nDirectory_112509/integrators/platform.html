  MedCommons® is a lightweight and easy to deploy platform for document management and medical imaging on the Web, available under license and as an On Demand Cloud Service. It is used by both doctors and patients.

This site is oriented towards systems integrators, oems, vars, and others who might deploy, embed and / or extend the MedCommons Platform. The on demand service runs at http://ondemand.medcommons.net Try it!

Federal mandates are now requiring patient control of medical records. To support and maintain permanent archival accounts over the lifetime of patients, the MedCommons Platform can be integrated into a healthcare customer's operations. This fault-tolerant and fully scalable repository is built upon standard data formats and common hardware and software interfaces, ensuring that the customer is always in a position to switch vendors of hospital systems.

The Federal Government is driving all health care providers to communicate in a consistent manner and are promoting free software for the connection of disparate healthcare systems and networks and suggesting its use in some federal solicitations. As the federal NHIN standards solidify MedCommons will allow its customers to route documents via the federal network to other institutions and individuals.

One Page Platform Brief
   
 
  Converts Fax to PDF for support of legacy communications  
  Creates standard structured (XML) medical records from DICOM and other formats for compatibility and uses these to organize imaging, reports and other documents for display and easy collaboration  
  Interfaces with NHIN, IHE and DICOM to collect and distribute documents  
  aggregates health records from different practices into a coherent and secure account for each patient  
  adds essential privacy and security features that help a client meet strict HIPAA and ARRA 2009 mandates for encryption  
  implements OpenID and OAuth standards for simple single sign-on and application integration  
  enhances applications and practices to meet the patient-facing and health information exchange requirements of ARRA "meaningful use"  
  supports numerous methods of notification and control over health records access including email, text message and iPhone app.  
  provides installation and management tools appropriate for public or private cloud installation, performance monitoring, backup and restore, security and help-desk operations  
  provides developer and integrator documentation and testing support at different levels of sophistication from simple HTML and scripting to sophisticated cloud APIs
