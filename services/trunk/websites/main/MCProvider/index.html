APP DESCRIPTION

MedCommons Provider is a free app for users of MedCommons Cloud Services.

View, Transfer, and Share MRI, CT Scans and other imaging via MedCommons

View, Transfer and Share National Health Information Network (NHIN) patient data

Receive notifications from MedCommons upon account activity

View dynamic patient lists

Add Photos and SOAP Notes to patient accounts

Take Fax directly into patient account

MedCommons provides secure access to cloud-based MedCommons Accounts.


Graphics by Martoons

 
Features:

  • Access MedCommons Groups from iPhone or iPad

  • View Inbox and permanent group Members

  • View individual patient HealthURLs

  • Preview and transfer standard DICOM

  • View and transfer federal-standard CCR health records

  • Share

  • Bar Coded Fax
