Put Your Patient's Data into Their Accounts

Use all of MedCommons for Your Patient's Benefit:

  • Your Front-Office Team can use HealthFrame to enter Patient Data
  • If you have a scanner you can use DDL
  • If you want to review a patient's records use the MedCommons Viewer
  • If you want to run a DICOM workstation, try Osirix here
  • Use a toll-free Fax Number to move paper documents into a patient's account

Remember, Fax and DICOM will cost your patients additional fees, which will be shared between MedCommons and your practice
