MedCommons develops next generation health care software that provides on-demand, real time, cloud computing solutions for electronic health record portals, hospitals and clinics, diagnostic imaging centers and sports medicine, telemedicine, disease management and patient advocacy service providers focused on meeting Federal Government and consumer demand for privacy, transparency, efficiency and coordination of electronic health records.

MedCommons portfolio of Software as a Service (SaaS) applications now enable the exchange of private and secure, comprehensive, integrated, electronic health records incorporating radiologist-ready diagnostic imaging as well as scanned original clinical documents. MedCommons is the first-ever, electronic health record solution designed expressly for private labeling, powering users' own existing capabilities to feature and differentiate their own trusted brand.
