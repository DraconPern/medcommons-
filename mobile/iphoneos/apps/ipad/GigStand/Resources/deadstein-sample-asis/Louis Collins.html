Louis Collins

 

Verse 1        Miz Collins weep Miz Collins mourn

                     To see your son Louis leave his home.

                     Angels laid him away.

Chorus         || :   Angels laid him away

                     They laid him six feet under the clay

                     Angels laid him away  :||

Verse 2        Oh, kind friends now ain't it hard

                     To see poor Louis in a new graveyard.

         Where angels laid him away.à Chorus

 

Verse 3        When they heard that Louis was dead

                     All the women folk dressed in red,

                     Cos angels have laid him away à Chorus

Verse 4    When they heard that Louis was dead

                 All the women folk dressed in red,

                 'Cos angels have laid him away.        à  Chorus

 

Verse 5    Bob shot one and Louis shot two

     But Barnes shot Collins, shot him through and through

                 Now angels have laid him away.       à  Chorus

 

Verse 6    Miz Collins weep, Miz Collins moan

     to see your son Louis leave his home -

angels laid him away. à  Chorus
