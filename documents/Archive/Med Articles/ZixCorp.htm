 Dear Adrian Gropper,

Please join us on Friday, January 9, 2004 for the January Focus-of-the-Month workshop. Guest Speaker Nicolas Nemer (Account Executive, ZixCorp) will present "ZixCorp: Easy-to-Use Solutions to Protect, Manage, and Deliver Secure e-Messaging". Even with the security regulation of HIPAA in effect, healthcare organizations may still unwittingly be exposing Protected Health Information (PHI) in their e- mail communications. Company guidelines are rarely enough to ensure HIPAA compliance or total end-user implementation. Comprehensive tools are available to help healthcare organizations identify e-mail vulnerabilities and to implement effective solutions that cover all areas of secure e-messaging protection including send-to-anyone encryption, automatic PHI content filtering, and anti-spam technology.

This presentation will discuss how ZixCorp approaches the challenge of enforcing company email security policies within healthcare organizations. ZixCorp addresses the problem with holistic e-messaging management services that identify e-mail security breaches - including PHI leaks, spam, and viruses - and help eliminate them with secure, policy-driven, enterprise-wide solutions that are easy to use, quick to implement, and help create successful, HIPAA compliant email security programs.
