Texas Docs Get a Secure Network

(January 15, 2004) Memorial Hermann Health Network Providers, Houston, has named Bluegate as its preferred vendor of secure Internet connectivity. Bluegate is a division of Crescent Communications Inc. of Houston. The provider network serves 2,900 independent physicians in 15 southeast Texas counties affiliated with Memorial Hermann Healthcare System. The arrangement will enable the physician group to establish a virtual private network among its members.
