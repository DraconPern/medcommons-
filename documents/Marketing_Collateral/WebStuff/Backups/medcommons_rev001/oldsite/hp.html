MedCommons is a service for secure digital transfer of patient health information records (PHI) among repositories and medical devices under explicit patient consent. Patients 'opt in' to PHI transfers by signing  online consent forms that preserve patient privacy.

MedCommons delivers increased productivity for physicians by serving as registrar and transfer agent, delivering clinical documents to healthcare professionals anywhere in the national information infrastructure digitally signed and provably authentic. Learn more...
