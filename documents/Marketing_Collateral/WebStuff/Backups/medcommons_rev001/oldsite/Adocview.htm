The MedCommons Gateway

Q - How do those fancy imaging studies get in and out of MedCommons? 
A - A button on (2) Encounter Form will take you to a list of DICOM studies on your very own MedCommons Gateway. The Gateway is a virtual device on your local network. It can appear as a DICOM workstation or a DICOM archive or both. It includes your own local network version of the MedCommons WADO service and document viewer. It's FDA approved Open Source Software for easier integration and peace-of-mind.

The MedCommons Gateway software will be available for free download and as as MedCommons supported eReferral appliance in June 2005. A notification will be sent to email addresses entered during the interactive demo.
